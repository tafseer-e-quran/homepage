Learn Arabic Language in the most simplest way to learn Quran. This for people who want to learn Quran in its own language (i.e. Arabic). Inshallah with this course everyone can learn Arabic grammer in this course.

Arabic Language is considered to be one of the most difficult languages to learn. Therefore, we will teach it as easily as possible so that everyone can understand.

We have set days for every topic. Every day you will be taught some part of Arabic Grammer [The syllabus is shown on the card/poster if you click/tap on it].

What you have to do :

You just have to learn these topic and practice them consistently. Practice everything we teach you.

First ten lectures are of Arabic Grammer
Next six lectures are of Arabic Vocabulary
Then comes translation and tafseer of Quranic Verses for you to learn, practice and remember.

Note: All of these three are important so don't miss any lecture.
